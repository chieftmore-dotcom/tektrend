About Dr. Bostone Emmanuel Neema

A dedicated spine surgeon committed to providing excellent spine care through innovative surgical techniques and compassionate patient relationships.

Professional Biography

Dr. Bostone Emmanuel Neema is a dedicated spine surgeon with extensive experience in spine surgery. He practices at Neema Medical Centre in Nairobi, Kenya, and provides locum services at Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital (MTRH) and Kenyatta National Hospital (KNH).

Dr. Neema studied medicine in India and began his surgical career there. He is committed to delivering high-quality spine care services, focusing on patient-centered care and innovative surgical approaches to improve health outcomes.

Beyond clinical practice, Dr. Neema is dedicated to advancing medical knowledge and mentoring future healthcare professionals. He is an alumnus of Friends School Kamusinga.
